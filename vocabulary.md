## Suggested replacements (from pitch feedback)

Words in your pitch you may want to swap for the improved versions:

| In the pitch | Replace with | Why |
|---|---|---|
| 无法理解 wúfǎ lǐjiě | 无法感知 wúfǎ gǎnzhī | "perceive" fits the thesis (AI can't sense space) better than "understand" |
| 实体业务 shítǐ yèwù | 现场工作 xiànchǎng gōngzuò / 实地操作 shídì cāozuò | reads less like corporate jargon |
| 独一无二的优势 dúyīwú'èr de yōushì | 独特的位置 dútè de wèizhì | less cliché, more concrete |
| 可是 kěshì | 然而 rán'ér | matches the formal register of the rest of the pitch |
